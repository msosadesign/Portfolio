Prototyping a Weather App

I consider this as one of my best projects, I was asked to design a high fidelity prototype of a Weather App. I wanted an experience that felt different, clean and efficient, and after looking for inspiration from other weather apps, I designed this.

Pet Finder Signup Form

Instant Pet Finder is an idea for an app that focuses on finding cute pets for cute users. I’ve been given the task to make the registration section on a wireframe. So I used design patters to make an enjoyable and engaging flow.

Usability Research

This one was tough, I have to admit, but it was worth it. Grocery Prime, a general supermarket, was considering adding an app to their shopping experience to help users fulfill their needs. My objective in this project was to uncover people’s problems in supermarkets to find a solution that would work with the app.
