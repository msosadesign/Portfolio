The Landing Fork Design Iterations

The Landing Fork has returned, and they want some Design Iterations. They liked the wireframe (yes!) so they wanted me to make a high fidelity version. The thing is, they wanted me to make a design based on tech-savvy elder people, and another design based on their baking products. For the tech-savvy elder audience, I implemented a bigger font size, some social media links to increase social engagement and filter search to help them find a specific topic. For the baking focused iteration, I dedicated the sidebar for this, making a “Popular Baking Toolkits” sidebar so that people can start baking with all their necessary tools.
