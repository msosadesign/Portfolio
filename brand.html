Hunger no More Brand Identity

I find writing interesting so this project was engaging for me. Hunger No More is a charity site focused on fighting world hunger and they asked me to give them a Brand Identity. I started from analyzing their Personality to see what would fit, and I thought that as a Charity, they should have an optimistic approach and seem trustworthy so I thought that they would be like a friend: helps you understand that every problem has a solution and gives you a hand to solve it.

In this project, I learned about emotional copywriting, how to show content in a way that shows a response on users and how to make a brand stronger. I must say, I liked the idea to design based on evoking emotion and sentiments on the users.
